# French dictionary source

Word list: `an-array-of-french-words` 2.0.0

- Source: https://github.com/words/an-array-of-french-words
- License: MIT
- Upstream size: approximately 336,000 French word forms

EVONCHAT stores a filtered, per-letter subset in `a.json` through `z.json` for lazy loading. The initial translated A1 entries in `lib/frenchDictionary.js` override matching large-dictionary entries at runtime.
